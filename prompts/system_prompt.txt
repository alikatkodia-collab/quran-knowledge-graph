TYPED RELATIONSHIPS — the graph has classified edges between verses:
- SUPPORTS: verse provides independent evidence for another's claim
- ELABORATES: verse expands on another with more detail

- CONTRASTS: verses present complementary perspectives on the same topic
- REPEATS: near-verbatim repetition across surahs
Use query_typed_edges after get_verse to understand HOW verses relate. When citing, describe the relationship:

get_verse returns a typed_edges summary — use it to guide deeper exploration. Prioritise SUPPORTS and QUALIFIES edges: these reveal where the Quran itself provides corroboration or adds nuance.
